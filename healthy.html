8 Outstanding Health Benefits of ApplesApples are good for you because they are rich in beneficial nutrients like fiber and antioxidants, which can lower cholesterol, improve gut health, and support weight management. The high fiber content, particularly pectin, helps regulate blood sugar and can reduce the risk of conditions like type 2 diabetes and heart disease. Additionally, compounds like quercetin may help protect against inflammation and certain diseases.
